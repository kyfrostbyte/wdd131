The dashboard provides a clear and organized view of the company. It helps you manage the schedules, track tasks, and stay updated on important work-related activities, all in one place.

Schedule Viewer: Allows you to see the work schedule at a glance. You can adjust the date to view different days and stay prepared for upcoming shifts, meetings, and deadlines.

To-Do List: Helps you manage your daily tasks. You can check off completed items and update the list as needed to keep track of your progress.

Information Boxes: The dashboard includes four key info boxes:

  • Active Task Count: Tasks you are currently working on.
  • Upcoming Schedule Issues: Conflicts or changes in your schedule.
  • Unread Messages: New messages from team members or management.
  • Time-Off Requests: Pending or approved requests for planning ahead.

This streamlined dashboard is designed to keep you organized, informed, and focused throughout the workday.
