Overview

Purpose

Uptown Management Tools is website designed to streamline many of the common tasks that managers must do. It is meant to both expedite and simplify tasks such as managing current staff, along with their skillsets and availabilities, creating daily work schedules, and accessing contact information for customers. Along with this, UMT has features for creating and tracking tasks, as well as displaying some statistical insights about the company.

Audience

The audience is employees that are in some form of managerial role for their company. Anyone that helps to plan out the details of the week, and helps to ensure that everything is in place for business to run would have access to the program.

Dynamic elements

Features such as the to-do list, daily schedules, staff profiles, and customer contact information will all be dynamically generated. This information will either be stored in JavaScript arrays, or maybe a lightweight DB, depending on how much information there is, and then the data will be rendered as needed.

Heading Font: Poppins

I chose the Poppins font because it is clean and clear. None of the letters have any potentially confusing characteristics. I also thought it was similar to Fira Sans, so they fit well together.

Paragraph Font: Fira Sans

I chose Fira Sans becausae I thought it had a nice modern look to it. It also seems to meet all of the accesibility rules. Overall, it felt like a very legible, orderly, and pleasing font.

Content

Home page

Text content:
Help section at the bottom:

The dashboard provides a clear and organized view of the company. It helps you manage the schedules, track tasks, and stay updated on important work-related activities, all in one place.

The Schedule Viewer allows you to see the work schedule at a glance. You can adjust the date to view different days and stay prepared for upcoming shifts, meetings, and deadlines.

The To-Do List helps you manage your daily tasks. You can check off completed items and update the list as needed to keep track of your progress.

The dashboard includes four key information boxes for quick updates. The Active Task Count box shows the number of tasks you are currently working on. The Upcoming Schedule Issues box alerts you to conflicts or changes in your schedule. The Unread Messages box shows the number of new messages from team members or management. The Time-Off Requests box displays pending or approved time-off requests to help you plan ahead.

This streamlined dashboard is designed to keep you organized, informed, and focused throughout the workday.

Staff

Text content:
Help section at the bottom:

The staff page provides a clear and organized overview of all staff members in one place. It allows managers to quickly access and update staff information as needed.

The page features a table that displays key details for each staff member, including first and last name, position, email, and phone number. This makes it easy to find and reference important contact and role information.

Each row includes an edit button that allows managers to update individual staff details directly from the table. This ensures that information remains accurate and up to date.

The table also includes a search function that allows managers to quickly locate specific staff members by name, position, or contact details. This makes it easy to find the right information without scrolling through the entire list.

The staff page is designed to provide a streamlined and efficient way for managers to manage staff information and keep records organized.

Schedule

Text content:

Help section at the botton:

The schedule page provides a detailed and organized view of the daily schedule. It helps you see upcoming shifts, breaks, and employee availability, making it easier to manage staffing and plan ahead.

The schedule shows two days at a time, giving you a clear overview of the current and next day's shifts. You can navigate through the schedule using the next and previous buttons, or jump to the current day with the "Today" button for quick access.

Each days schedule provides a detailed breakdown of employee shifts and break times. This allows you to see who is working and when, helping you avoid conflicts and ensure proper coverage.

The page also includes an Edit Button that allows you to adjust the schedule as needed. You can update shift times, assign breaks, and make other changes to keep the schedule accurate and up to date.

The schedule page is designed to give you a clear and flexible view of employee schedules, helping you manage shifts and staffing with confidence.
