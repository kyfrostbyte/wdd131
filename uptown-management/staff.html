The staff page provides a clear and organized overview of all staff members in one place. It allows managers to quickly access and update staff information as needed.

Staff Table: Displays key details for each staff member, including first and last name, position, email, and phone number. This makes it easy to find and reference important contact and role information.

Edit Button: Each row includes an edit icon that allows managers to update individual staff details directly from the table, helping ensure the information stays accurate and up to date.

Search Function: Managers can quickly locate specific staff members by name, position, or contact details using the search input above the table.

Sort & Filter Options: Use the available buttons to sort by name or position, or filter by role to narrow down the list and focus on specific groups.

This streamlined staff page is designed to help managers efficiently manage staff information, keep records organized, and access key details at a glance.
